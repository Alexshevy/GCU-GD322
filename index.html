Buy and flip (new items)

When buying and flipping new items, it’s good to know that many stores, such as Walmart, Target, Home Depot, TJ Maxx, etc., have apps in which items can be scanned on the phone and the cost for each will be shown. Also, another great website to use when buying and flipping is Brickseat.com. This site shows discounted prices at store locations nearest to you. What most people do not realize is that many items are listed as one price in the store and as a lesser price on the app. For example, a product might retail as $39.98 in the Walmart store, but once scanned it may come up as $4.00. At this point, you go to your Amazon app, scan the product to see how much its selling for (let’s just say $35.99), buy the product, resell it through Amazon, and make a $31.99 profit per item. A tip I would give is to buy as many of the item that the store has in its stock and resell all of them on Amazon.

In-Store Scanning:

Step 1: Download the store’s app (Example: If you’re at Walmart, then download the Walmart app)

Step 2: Go to the clearance aisle and scan items to see the current discounts

Step 3: If you find a good item that has a good discount, then search for it on Amazon to see their selling price.

Step 4: If the profit is worth it, then purchase as many of the item as there are and check out.

Step 5: Set up an Amazon seller account and sell through Amazon.

Online Scanning:

Step 1: Go to Brickseat.com and put in your zip code. Once that is completed, a list of local stores with clearance items will be given.

Step 2: Go to the store. Search for the item on Amazon to see the selling price.

Step 3: Decide if the profit is worth it. If so, make the purchase.

Step 4: Set up an Amazon seller account and sell through Amazon.
